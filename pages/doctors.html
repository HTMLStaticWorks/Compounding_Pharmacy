Sterile Compounding

Accepted Prescription Workflows

We support multiple secure, HIPAA-compliant channels for clinical teams to transmit compounding orders.

E-Prescribing (EHR)

Transmit directly through your electronic health record (EHR) system. Search for 'CompoundRx Pharmacy' located in New York, NY, and send standard e-scripts directly from Epic, Cerner, or eClinicalWorks.

HIPAA Secure Fax

Fax physical compounding order sheets directly to our secure, dedicated intake machine at +1-800-555-0124. Clinicians can request printed template order forms by contacting our support team.

Verbal & Written Scripts

Call our licensed compounding pharmacists directly at our peer line to leave a verbal prescription. Alternatively, hand written compounding slips can be given to patients to present at our office.
