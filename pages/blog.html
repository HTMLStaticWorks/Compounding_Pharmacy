FAQ

Common Questions

What is the typical turnaround time for custom formulations?

Most custom compounded medications are prepared, checked for dosage accuracy, and verified in our laboratory within 24 to 48 hours. Shipping takes an additional 1-3 business days depending on delivery selection.

Do you accept insurance for compounded medications?

While many insurance providers offer compounding coverage, it varies. We provide detailed universal claim forms (reimbursement receipts) containing specific drug codes for direct patient reimbursement submissions.

How can my clinical practice submit prescriptions?

Clinicians can locate 'CompoundRx' in their standard EHR directory to prescribe electronically, fax completed prescription forms to +1-800-555-0124, or upload scans through this contact portal.

Are your active compounding ingredients FDA-approved?

We source all raw active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) from FDA-registered and regulated chemical distributors. Every component is accompanied by a certified CoA verifying chemical purity.
